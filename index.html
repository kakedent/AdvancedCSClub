About Us

The Advanced Academy Coding Club is a student-led organization that aims to promote interest in computer science and provide a supportive community for learners of all levels. We offer a variety of events, including workshops, hackathons, and guest speaker sessions, as well as resources such as online tutorials and problem sets. Our club is open to students of all majors and experience levels, and we welcome anyone who is interested in learning more about coding and technology. Join us today and take your skills to the next level!
